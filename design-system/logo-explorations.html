VettdRE — Logo & Brand Exploration

Modern tech-forward · Pure black + electric accent · Abstract architectural · Industrial typography

Logo Explorations
Option A — Skyline Angles
Skyline Angles. Five building forms at varying heights create an abstract Manhattan skyline silhouette. The teal baseline represents the ground/foundation. Tall, condensed Bebas Neue wordmark echoes the verticality of the buildings. "RE" in accent teal.
Option B — Negative Space V
Negative Space V. Two building towers form a "V" through the negative space between them — a canyon of buildings like a NYC avenue. Teal accent at the base and a spire detail. Oswald wordmark (medium weight) for a slightly less condensed industrial feel. Architectural but cleverly encodes the brand initial.
Option C — Grid Blueprint
Grid Blueprint. Wireframe buildings evoke architectural blueprints and technical precision. Floor lines suggest data layers. A small verification checkmark in teal ties to the "Vettd" name. Two-line wordmark stacks "VETTD" (bold, wide-tracked) over "REAL ESTATE" (light, ultra-tracked) for a clean lockup. Most technical/data-forward of the three.
